Fasten Health TEFCA IAS Beta

Connect to TEFCA and retrieve the clinical records that belong to you. Our widget lets you authenticate with participating healthcare organizations, pull a fresh copy of your health data, and download it to keep—Fasten never stores the payload.

  • Secure, patient-driven access to the TEFCA network
  • Stateless retrieval—download your records directly after each export
  • Guided onboarding to collect the providers that know you best
  • Export-ready records to power second opinions, care coordination, and more

Because the beta is stateless, you stay in control: once the TEFCA network responds, you will be prompted to download everything, and Fasten does not retain a copy on our servers.
