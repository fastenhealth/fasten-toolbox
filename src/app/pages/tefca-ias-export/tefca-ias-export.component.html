Access your medical records

Experience the power of the Fasten Connect platform and pull your own medical records in just a few steps.

  1. Click the Export Records button to begin, and enter your personal email address.
  2. Next, check your email for a 6 digit code from Fasten.
  3. Click Verify with Clear and complete the Clear verification steps. You will be prompted to enter your phone number, take a Selfie and potentially take a photo of your government issued id.
  4. Once you return, wait 2 minutes while Fasten attempts to find healthcare providers that you have previously visited, that are live on the TEFCA network
  5. If we find any providers, you can check a box (or Sign In) to consent and share those records
  6. Click Continue and Close
  7. On success, you can view & download your records in FHIR format.
Demo Limitations
  • This demo is essentially stateless & your data is only cached for 24h.
  • Refreshing the page will require re-authentication with your provider.
  • You can only export one provider at a time; repeat the process for additional providers.
  • Exports can take 5+ minutes if you have a long medical history.
  • Some sources may not be working correctly.
  • Exports larger than 10MB may fail or slow down your browser. Contact us if you need help retrieving a large payload.
These limitations only exist in this stateless demo environment, your actual integration will not have these limitations
